A clothes hanger is a device in the shape of human shoulders designed to facilitate the hanging of your clothes. These have a simple loop of wire, most often steel that continues into the shape of a flattened triangle. There are also wooden hangers that have on the bottom half a piece of wood cut in the shape of a boomerang.
